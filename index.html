The HIIT Protocol

Your work-to-rest ratio determines which energy system you train. The 2kg System uses two ratios — one for fat loss, one for conditioning.

40/20

Fat Loss

40s on, 20s off. Aerobic + anaerobic hybrid. Burns fat while building endurance.

20/10

Conditioning

20s on, 10s off. Maximal power, glycogen burn. Builds speed and explosive strength.

Every 2kg you lose, your work capacity increases and your ratios need to shift. The 2kg System auto-adjusts — generic programs don't, which is why you plateau.

The Metabolism Reset

Every 2kg, your body adapts. Metabolism slows. Hunger signals change. This is not failure — it's biology. The reset protocol prevents the crash.

7-Day Reset Protocol

Days 1–3: Eat at maintenance calories, light walking only.
Days 4–5: Return to deficit, full HIIT.
Days 6–7: Normal training.

By Day 7 your metabolism is recalibrated for the next 2kg. Rest is not weakness. It's strategy.

The 35-Min Ceiling

You don't need more time. You need more density. The 2kg System caps every session at 35 minutes — max.

Progress doesn't come from adding minutes. It comes from shorter rest periods, more work in the same window, and increased intensity. If you can go longer than 35 minutes, you're not working hard enough.

Density over duration. Always.
